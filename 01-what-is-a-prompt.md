# Welcome to Your Blog 💛

Hey Esther, hey Sarah.

I made this little corner of the internet just for you two.

No rules here. No grades. No one looking over your shoulder.

This is your space to write whatever is on your heart — your thoughts, your feelings, the funny things that happened today, the things you can't quite say out loud, the dreams you're still figuring out.

Some days you might write a lot. Some days just a sentence. Some days nothing at all. That's completely okay.

---

## Why I made this for you

I know life gets busy and we don't always get to sit down and talk the way I'd like to.

I hope this blog can be a place where you put things down — not for anyone else, but for yourself.

Writing has a way of helping us understand what we're feeling, even when things feel confusing or hard.

And one day, when you look back at these posts, I think you'll be glad you wrote them.

---

## This is completely yours

What you write here belongs to you.

You can write about anything — big things, small things, happy things, hard things.

You don't have to be a good writer. You just have to be honest.

I love you both so much. 🧡

— Mum
