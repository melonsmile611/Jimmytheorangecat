About This Blog 🧡

🐱

Made by Mum, for Esther & Sarah 🧡

This is a private blog made with love — a safe little space just for the two of you to write freely.

Why I made this for you

I know life gets busy and we don't always get to sit down and talk the way I'd like to. I hope this blog can be a place where you put things down — not for anyone else, but for yourself.

Writing has a way of helping us understand what we're feeling, even when things feel confusing or hard.

This is completely yours

What you write here belongs to you. You can write about anything — big things, small things, happy things, hard things. You don't have to be a good writer. You just have to be honest.

I love you both so much. 🧡 — Mum
